Wheatgrass

Wheatgrass is a supplement used to support detoxification and overall health in dogs, often used for its antioxidant properties.

DOSAGE CALCULATOR

(1-2 grams per 10 kg daily)

PRECAUTIONS

Gradually introduce to avoid digestive upset; consult a vet before use if the dog has wheat allergies.

USES

  • Detoxification
  • Overall Health
  • Antioxidant Support

POTENTIAL SIDE EFFECTS

  • Diarrhea
  • Gas
  • Stomach Upset
